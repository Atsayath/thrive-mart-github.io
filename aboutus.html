About Us

At Thrive Mart, we pride ourselves on being Sri Lanka's premier destination for fresh, high-quality groceries. Established with a passion for delivering the finest produce to our valued customers, we offer an extensive selection of fresh fruits, vibrant vegetables, nourishing dairy products, and indulgent cosmetics to elevate your shopping experience.

With a commitment to sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers, we ensure that every item on our shelves meets the highest standards of quality and freshness. From succulent tropical fruits to crisp, farm-fresh vegetables, each product is carefully selected to bring the essence of Sri Lanka's rich agricultural heritage straight to your table.

Convenience is key, which is why we have strategically located our stores in eight accessible locations across Sri Lanka. Whether you're in the bustling heart of Colombo or the serene landscapes of Kandy, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to assist you in finding the perfect ingredients for your culinary creations.

At Thrive Mart, we believe in fostering a sense of community and connection through food. As a proudly Sri Lankan-owned and operated business, we are deeply rooted in our local culture and traditions. We strive to create a shopping environment that celebrates diversity, inclusivity, and the vibrant tapestry of Sri Lankan cuisine.

From the moment you step into our stores, you'll be greeted with the aroma of freshly harvested produce and the warm smiles of our dedicated team. Whether you're a seasoned chef, a health-conscious consumer, or simply someone who appreciates the pleasure of good food, we invite you to experience the difference at Thrive Mart. Join us on a journey of culinary discovery, where every visit is an opportunity to indulge in the bounty of Sri Lanka's natural abundance.

Our Branches

Colombo Branch

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Colombo, our downtown branch provides an extensive array of fresh produce, organic products, and gourmet groceries. Experience the hustle and bustle of the city with extended shopping hours and a dedicated team eager to help you with all your grocery needs.

Kandy Branch

Located in the picturesque hills of Kandy, our branch offers a serene shopping experience with a wide variety of fresh produce, organic products, and gourmet groceries. Enjoy the charm of Kandy with convenient shopping hours and a friendly staff ready to assist you in finding the finest selections.
